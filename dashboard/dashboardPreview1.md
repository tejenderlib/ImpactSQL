# 📊 Page 1 — Overview Dashboard

This dashboard provides a high-level overview of global disaster impact, including human loss, economic damage, and trends over time.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

The goal of this dashboard is to:

* Summarize global disaster impact using key metrics
* Identify trends over time
* Highlight high-risk disaster types and regions

---

## 📊 Components

### 🔹 KPI Cards

* Total Deaths
* Total Affected Population
* Total Economic Damage
* Total Disaster Events

👉 Provides a quick summary of global impact.

---

### 🔹 Year-wise Trend (Bar Chart)

* Shows total deaths per year

👉 Helps identify spikes and long-term trends.

---

### 🔹 Disaster Type Distribution (Pie Chart)

* Shows contribution of each disaster type

👉 Highlights the most impactful disaster categories.

---

### 🔹 Top Countries (Table)

* Displays countries with highest economic damage

👉 Identifies regions with significant financial impact.

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* Earthquakes are the deadliest disaster type
* Economic damage exceeds $3.93 trillion globally
* Disaster impact shows increasing trends over time
* Certain countries consistently experience high losses

---

## ⚙️ Filters

* Region filter
* Year filter

👉 Enables interactive exploration of data.

---

## 🧠 Notes

* Data is aggregated from EM-DAT dataset
* Economic damage values were normalized from ('000 US$) to USD
* Dashboard is optimized for clarity and performance
